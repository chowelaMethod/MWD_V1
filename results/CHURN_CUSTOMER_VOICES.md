## üéØ Key Themes Across All Customer Feedback

### 1. **QuickBooks Is Both Strength and Weakness**
- Tight QB integration attracts customers
- But when QB has issues, Method becomes unusable
- When customers outgrow QB, they leave Method too
- No standalone value proposition

### 2. **Sales Teams Struggle Most**
- Reporting/dashboards insufficient
- Mobile experience too slow/clunky
- Field reps find it cumbersome
- Not "sales-first" CRM

### 3. **Customization Paradox**
- Out-of-box product insufficient for many use cases
- But customization is too hard and too expensive
- Customers want self-service customization tools
- Long customization timelines kill momentum

### 4. **Performance Kills Adoption**
- Slow page loads
- Frequent freezing
- White screens
- Sync issues with QB
- Mobile performance especially bad

### 5. **Onboarding Is Too Hard**
- Takes too long to get value
- Requires too much customer effort
- Customers get stuck in setup phase
- Need better self-service onboarding

### 6. **Competitive Threats Are Real**
- Losing to: Salesforce, NetSuite, HubSpot, MS Dynamics
- Competitors offer better reporting, UX, and integrations
- Enterprise customers graduate to full ERP platforms
- SMBs find more modern/easier alternatives
